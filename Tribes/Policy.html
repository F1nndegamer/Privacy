Privacy Policy

Developer: Finn Nijpels — Last updated: 23-11-2025

1. Summary

Short version: Tribes does not collect, store, or process personal data. This policy explains that more fully.

2. Information Collected

We do not collect personal information. Specifically:

  • No emails or usernames.
  • No analytics or usage tracking.
  • No device identifiers, IP addresses, or telemetry.
  • No chat or player logs.

3. Data Storage

Because we do not collect data, nothing is stored on any server or cloud service related to player activity.

4. Third-Party Services

Tribes uses no third-party analytics, ad networks, or tracking services.

5. Children

Tribes is available to users under 18. As no personal data is collected, there is minimal privacy risk. Parents/guardians should supervise younger players where appropriate.

6. GDPR & Your Rights

Although you have rights under data protection laws (access, rectification, deletion, objection), we hold no personal data, so those rights cannot be exercised in practice. If you believe data related to you is stored somewhere, contact us and we will investigate.

7. Security

No personal data is collected or stored, so there is no personal data risk from the game itself.

8. Changes to this Policy

This policy may be updated. Changes will be posted where the Privacy Policy is available (for example, on the game website or Discord).
